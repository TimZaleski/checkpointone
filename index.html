The Highest Preforming Specialization in Shadowlands?

The sims don't lie, Outlaw is doing very well in the meters these days. The last minute changes to our Covenant abilities as well as a very powerful legendary item have thrust us to the front of the line. Celerity being a dungeon drop makes it obtainable by the second week of Shadowland's release, which is a welcome boost to early mythics and raiding.

Between the two other choices, assassination is the clear loser. The 'dot' oriented spec has hemorrhaged usability early on, especially due to its weak AOE capabilities. Subtlety at least can spam Fan of Knives and try to imitate Blade Flurry, but it will always fall short.

I vividly remember the Legion preview panel at Blizzcon 2015, and the PowerPoint slide that would alter my destiny. The developers were discussing the new systems of the upcoming expansion, one of which was an "artifact weapon" that would be tailored to each class and specialization. Unlocking the weapons themselves was a quest chain, and the player would spend the rest of the expansion increasing its power. For these weapons, however, you weren't locked in to just one look. They came in a variety of skins and models, each gated behind an achievement or quest. I was blown away by the designs being presented on the screen. These models simply looked incredible, and when I saw the fencing style weapon, I knew that I had to unlock it. This weapon has been part of my signature look for years.
